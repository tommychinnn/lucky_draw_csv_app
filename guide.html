Common Questions

Q: What file formats are supported?

A: We support CSV and text files. CSV files should have one name per line.

Q: Can I save my draw results?

A: Yes, you can export your draw results in various formats.

Q: Is there a limit to the number of participants?

A: The free version supports up to 1000 participants. Contact us for larger events.
